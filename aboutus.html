Who We Are

Carbon Footprint Tracker is a tool built to help individuals monitor and reduce their daily carbon emissions. Our mission is to encourage sustainable habits by giving people actionable insights into their environmental impact.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology empowers every person to make eco-conscious choices effortlessly. We aim to spread environmental awareness through data-driven decisions.
